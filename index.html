About Us

Roni Analytics is an innovative data analytics startup that is harnessing the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) with a goal of bringing reliable and comprehensive insights on financial markets to users.

Roni AI is our proprietary tool that draws information on financial markets from multiple sources and provides intelligent insights, predictions, and forecasts about them, in four defined formats. We aim to enable users to protect their investments and make better decisions that in turn helps the financial ecosystem.

Why Us

Roni AI utilises financial markets’ datasets to design fundamental and technical metrics and visualisation dashboards that contain information on market developments, predictions, forecasting, risk analysis on stocks, currencies, and digital assets.

Our offering is distinguished because we use proprietary AI algorithms to develop the metrics. In fact, Roni AI keeps a book of habits on 800+ wallets of known market movers, such as:

• Technicals - Open interest, shorts vs longs, crypto deposited on exchanges, liquidity pools
• 80+ Network metrics - Wallet growth, transaction volumes, NVT
• Miner Health - Miner transactions, hash rate, difficulty adjustments
• On-chain Flows - Exchange, wallet, inter-entity flows
• Market Dislocations - Pricing of various markets to find inefficiencies
• Market Sentiment - Gauge trader feelings of various cryptos

These metrics due to their unique nature and involving no manual intervention, provide an edge to users over other tools and reports available in the industry.
